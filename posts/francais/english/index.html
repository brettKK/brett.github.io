给自己争取时间去思考 buy yourself some time 🔗

不要一上来就心急火燎的去回答这个问题。先跟问问题的人互动一下。顺便给自己争取点时间去思考。

step1： acknowledge or compliment the question. 夸奖或者确认这个问题。 that is an excellent question, or that is a very important question, or i really like this question.

step2: inform others you need time to think. verbalize 把需要时间思考这个点说出来。i need a few seconds to think about it. let me think about it for a minute.
